# Project Brief

## Name

ClinicFlow AI

## Positioning

ClinicFlow AI is an AI workflow assistant for small clinics, solo practices, and service teams. It converts fragmented intake materials into structured operational outputs.

## Inputs

- chat logs
- intake forms
- screenshots or image-based documents
- voice transcription text
- ad hoc user requirements

## Outputs

- structured case summary
- action checklist
- timeline draft
- risk reminders
- editable follow-up document

## Why this is a useful AI project

The target scenario is realistic and high-frequency. Many small teams do not need a giant enterprise system, but they do need a faster way to process messy operational information. The project emphasizes execution-oriented outputs instead of generic chatbot responses.

## Planned AI capabilities

- long-context understanding
- structured extraction
- multi-step task planning
- multimodal intake processing
- repeated regression testing for prompt and workflow quality
